Dell Precision M3800 Workstation

dell laptops linux

Dell’s Precision M3800 is an ultrabook-shaped mobile workstation, combining powerful components with a slim chassis.

I’ve owned this laptop for a number of years now, I rebuilt this unit after being given it by a friend who purchased a newer laptop after a mainboard & internal battery failure. I replaced the motherboard & battery; roughly $300nzd / $180usd is what it cost me, additionally I purchased a sata adapter cable since this didn’t come with one included.

I installed a 2TB SATA SSD, after installing this SATA Adapter (PN: DG95V). Note: Ensure you have a 61WH Battery, as a 91WH battery wont allow space for the SSD.

  • AliExpress M3800 Mainboard
  • eBay T0TRM 61Wh Battery
  • SATA Adapter (PN: DG95V)

Linux

eBay listings for this laptop make it a good decent proposition, especially if you pick up a 4K and/or 4K touch SKU, as a great Linux laptop. Using the SATA cable adapter, you can install the OS on the mSATA drive, mounting the SATA SSD on the adapter on /home.

Linux support on the Precision M3800 is really quite good, as it was one of the first retail developer edition that received official OEM Ubuntu Linux support1.
